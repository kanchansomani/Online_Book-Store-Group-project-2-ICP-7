Types of Books Available
Fiction

Fiction books are imaginative works of literature that are not based on real events or people. They are created by the author's imagination and can include genres such as fantasy, science fiction, romance, mystery, and historical fiction. Fiction books aim to entertain, engage, and transport readers to different worlds or scenarios.

Non-fiction

Non-fiction books are factual works that are based on real events, people, or information. They cover a wide range of topics such as history, science, biographies, self-help, travel, and true crime. Non-fiction books aim to inform, educate, or persuade readers about a particular subject or issue.

Mystery

Mystery and thriller books are characterized by suspenseful plots, unexpected twists, and thrilling elements. Mystery books focus on solving a puzzle or uncovering a secret, while thriller books create tension and excitement through fast-paced action and danger

Romance

Romance books focus on romantic relationships and love stories between characters. They explore themes of love, passion, intimacy, and emotional connections. Romance novels can be set in various time periods or settings and often have a happy ending where the main characters find love and happiness together.

Science Fiction

Science fiction and fantasy books are speculative genres that explore imaginative worlds, futuristic technology, magic, and other fantastical elements. Science fiction often delves into scientific concepts, technology advancements, and futuristic societies, while fantasy focuses on magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests

Historical Fiction

Historical fiction books are set in the past and often blend real historical events with fictional characters and storylines. These books transport readers to different time periods and cultures, offering a glimpse into historical events, social norms, and customs.
